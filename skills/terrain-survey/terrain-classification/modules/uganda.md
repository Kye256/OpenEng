## M.2 Country-Specific Knowledge

### M.2.1 Uganda Terrain Thresholds

The MoWT Road Design Manual defines terrain based on **transverse terrain slope** -- the slope of the ground measured perpendicular to the road centreline.

| Terrain Class | Transverse Slope | Description |
|---------------|-----------------|-------------|
| Flat | Around 5% | Level or gently rolling country which offers few obstacles to the construction of a road having continuously unrestricted horizontal and vertical alignment |
| Rolling | > 5% to 20% | Rolling, hilly or foothill country where the slopes generally rise and fall moderately gently and where occasional steep slopes may be encountered. It will offer some restrictions in horizontal and vertical alignment |
| Mountainous | > 20% to 70% | Rugged, hilly and mountainous country and river gorges. This class of terrain imposes definite restrictions on the standard of alignment obtainable and often involves long steep grades and limited sight distances |
| Escarpment | > 70% | Extreme slopes where switchback road alignments or side hill traverse sections with very large earthwork quantities are required. Standards associated with the other terrain types cannot be met |

**Source:** MoWT Road Design Manual, Vol. 1, Section 5.2 (Verified from PDF)

**Note on threshold interpretation:** The MoWT text describes flat terrain as "around 5%" and rolling as "20% >= transverse terrain slope > 5%". The thresholds are approximate guides -- engineering judgment applies at the boundaries. These thresholds differ from AASHTO values (which typically use 10%, 25%, 60%) because the MoWT manual is calibrated for Ugandan conditions.

### M.2.2 Design Speed Implications by Terrain

Terrain class directly determines design speed via the MoWT design tables. For reference (full tables in Skill #13 Uganda module):

| Design Class | Flat | Rolling | Mountainous |
|-------------|------|---------|-------------|
| Ia Paved | 120 km/h | 100 km/h | 80 km/h |
| Ib Paved | 110 km/h | 100 km/h | 80 km/h |
| II Paved | 90 km/h | 70 km/h | 60 km/h |
| III Paved | 80 km/h | 70 km/h | 50 km/h |
| A Gravel | 90 km/h | 80 km/h | 70 km/h |
| B Gravel | 80 km/h | 60 km/h | 50 km/h |
| C Gravel | 60 km/h | 50 km/h | 40 km/h |

**Source:** MoWT Vol. 1, Tables 4-3 through 4-9 (see Skill #13 Uganda module for full parameter tables)
