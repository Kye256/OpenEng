## M.1 Standards & Institutional Context

### M.1.1 Applicable Standards

| Standard | Edition/Year | Sections Referenced | Status |
|----------|-------------|---------------------|--------|
| PPDA Standard Bidding Documents for Procurement of Works | 2014 edition (updated periodically) | All sections | Current for GoU-funded |
| World Bank Standard Bidding Documents: Procurement of Works | 2017 edition | All sections | Current for WB-funded |
| PPDA (Rules and Methods) Regulations 2014 | SI No. 8 of 2014 | Regulations 23-40 (Bidding Documents) | Current |
| MoWT General Specifications for Road and Bridge Works | Current edition | Referenced in technical specifications | Current |

### M.1.2 Institutional Context

**PPDA Standard Bidding Documents:** The PPDA Authority issues authorised SBDs for each procurement type (Section 62 of PPDA Act, Regulation 23). PDEs must use these documents. Any deviation requires PPDA Authority approval (Regulation 10 of PPDA Regulations 2014). The PPDA issues different SBDs for different procurement methods (open bidding, restricted bidding, quotation) and types (works, supplies, services).

**World Bank Standard Bidding Documents:** For WB-funded projects, the World Bank issues its own SBDs. The WB SBD for Procurement of Works contains modified FIDIC conditions and mandatory World Bank clauses (fraud/corruption, inspection/audit, eligibility). UNRA uses WB SBDs for all WB-funded road projects.

**Document Approval:** Regulation 23(3) requires basic rules for bidding document preparation: (a) changes to ITB only through BDS entries, (b) changes to GCC only through PCC entries, (c) statement of requirements may be modified as required. The Contracts Committee must approve all bidding documents before issue (Section 29(a)(ii)).

### M.1.3 Standard Philosophy

The PPDA approach emphasises standardisation -- PDEs use prescribed templates and customise only through designated fields (BDS, PCC). This reduces errors, ensures legal compliance, and makes evaluation consistent. Departures from SBDs require PPDA Authority approval.

| GoU | Open International Bidding | PPDA SBD for Works (OIB) | FIDIC Red Book with PPDA PCC template |
| GoU | Restricted Bidding | PPDA SBD for Works (Restricted) | FIDIC Red Book with PPDA PCC template |

### M.2.2 Key BDS Entries for Uganda Road Projects

**Standard BDS entries that must be completed for each UNRA road project:**

| BDS Entry | Typical Content | Notes |
|-----------|----------------|-------|
| Employer name | Uganda National Roads Authority (UNRA) or District Local Government | Full legal name |
| Procurement reference | UNRA/WORKS/[year]/[number] | PPDA reference numbering system |
| Bid validity period | 90-120 days | 90 days standard for UNRA |
| Bid security | 2% of estimated value, bank guarantee | From commercial bank acceptable to Employer |
| Evaluation method | Technical compliance (pass/fail) then lowest evaluated price | Standard for works per PPDA Evaluation Regs |
| Submission deadline | Allow minimum bidding period per Regulation 46 | 40 working days minimum for works (open bidding) |
| Bid currency | Uganda Shillings (UGX), with provision for foreign currency components | Dual currency for international bidding |
| Margin of preference | As specified in BDS | May apply for domestic bidders per PPDA provisions |

### M.2.3 Particular Conditions for UNRA Projects

**Standard modifications in UNRA Particular Conditions (project-specific -- always verify):**

| Sub-Clause | Standard Provision | Typical UNRA Modification |
|------------|-------------------|--------------------------|
| 1.1.6 (Contract Price) | Lump sum or re-measurement | Re-measurement (standard for road works) |
| 4.2 (Performance Security) | As per contract | 10% of Contract Price, bank guarantee |
| 8.2 (Time for Completion) | As stated in Appendix | Project-specific (typically 18-36 months for road projects) |
| 8.7 (Delay Damages) | Rate per day | 0.05-0.10% of Contract Price per day |
| 14.2 (Advance Payment) | As stated | 10-15% of Contract Price, repayable from 10% cumulative IPC |
| 14.7 (Payment) | 56 days | Sometimes extended to 60-90 days for GoU projects |
| 14.9 (Retention) | As stated | 5% retention to 5% maximum |
| 20.2 (DAB) | 1 or 3 members | 1 member for contracts < UGX 20B, 3 members above |
| 20.6 (Arbitration) | ICC Rules | UNCITRAL Rules in some UNRA contracts, seat Kampala |

### M.2.4 Technical Specifications References

For UNRA road projects, technical specifications typically reference:

1. **MoWT General Specifications for Road and Bridge Works** -- Primary reference for materials, workmanship, and testing requirements
2. **Project-specific amendments** -- Additional or modified specifications for the particular project
3. **Environmental and social management requirements** -- Increasingly required, especially for donor-funded projects
4. **Safety specifications** -- Site safety requirements

### M.2.5 Bidding Period Requirements

From PPDA (Rules and Methods) Regulations 2014, Regulation 46:

| Method | Minimum Bidding Period | Notes |
|--------|----------------------|-------|
| Open Domestic Bidding | 20 working days (supplies), 30 working days (works) | From date of first publication or availability of documents |
| Open International Bidding | 30 working days (supplies), 40 working days (works) | From date of first publication internationally |
| Restricted Bidding | 15 working days (supplies), 20 working days (works) | From date of issue of documents to shortlisted bidders |
| Quotation | 5-10 working days (typical) | No minimum specified in regulations |
| Prequalification | 15 working days (domestic), 20 working days (international) | Regulation 20 |

## M.3 Country-Specific Reasoning

### M.3.1 Decision Logic (Country Branch)

**Uganda SBD Selection:**
```
Is the project GoU-funded or donor-funded?
  |-- GoU funded:
  |     Is it Open Bidding (> UGX 500M)?
  |       |-- YES: PPDA SBD for Works (ODB or OIB)
  |       +-- NO: PPDA Quotation Forms or Restricted Bidding SBD
  |
  +-- World Bank funded:
        Is it ICB (> USD 15M)?
          |-- YES: World Bank SBD for Procurement of Works
          +-- NO (NCB): World Bank SBD or acceptable PPDA SBD
                         (WB reviews PPDA SBD for acceptability)
```

### M.3.2 Professional Judgment (Country-Specific)

**PPDA SBDs are mandatory and may not be modified without PPDA Authority approval.** This is a strict requirement. Using non-standard documents or modifying standard text (as opposed to using BDS/PCC) is a procurement violation that may be identified during PPDA audit.

**For World Bank-funded UNRA projects, World Bank SBDs are usually used.** Even for NCB, the WB SBD is preferred because it includes mandatory WB clauses. If PPDA SBDs are used for NCB, they must include WB mandatory clauses as an addendum.

**Cross-check BoQ items against MoWT standard specifications.** The BoQ item descriptions and measurement units should be consistent with MoWT specifications. Inconsistencies between BoQ and specifications are the most common tender document quality issue in UNRA projects.

**Environmental and social requirements are increasingly important.** World Bank-funded projects require Environmental and Social Impact Assessment (ESIA) documentation and compliance requirements in the tender documents. This is typically included as an annex to the technical specifications.

### M.3.3 Common Errors (Country-Specific)

1. **Using old edition PPDA SBDs.** PPDA updates SBDs periodically. Always verify the current edition from PPDA Authority.
2. **Omitting World Bank mandatory clauses from NCB documents.** If PPDA SBDs are used for WB-funded NCB, the mandatory WB clauses must be included.
3. **Insufficient bidding period.** Not allowing the minimum bidding period specified in the regulations.
4. **Inconsistent Particular Conditions.** PCC modifications that contradict BDS entries or create ambiguities.
5. **Missing Contracts Committee approval.** Bidding documents must be approved by the Contracts Committee before issue (Section 29(a)(ii)).

## M.5 Country-Specific Validation

### M.5.1 Range Checks

| Parameter | Minimum | Maximum | Unit | Standard Reference | Action if Violated |
|-----------|---------|---------|------|-------------------|-------------------|
| Bidding period (ODB works) | 30 | No max | Working days | Regulation 46 | Below minimum is a procurement violation |
| Bidding period (OIB works) | 40 | No max | Working days | Regulation 46 | Below minimum is a procurement violation |
| Bid security | 1 | 3 | % of estimate | PPDA SBD | If outside range, verify with SBD |

### M.5.2 Standards Compliance Checks

- **Check:** SBD is current edition issued by PPDA Authority (GoU) or World Bank
- **Standard:** PPDA Act Section 62, Regulation 23
- **Pass condition:** Edition matches current published version
- **Fail action:** NOTE -- Verify SBD edition from PPDA Authority or World Bank

- **Check:** Contracts Committee approval obtained before issue
- **Standard:** PPDA Act Section 29(a)(ii)
- **Pass condition:** Evidence of Contracts Committee approval
- **Fail action:** STOP -- Bidding documents must be approved before issue

- **Check:** Changes to ITB only through BDS entries
- **Standard:** Regulation 23(3)(a)
- **Pass condition:** Standard ITB text unmodified; all changes via BDS
- **Fail action:** SENIOR REVIEW -- Standard ITB text appears modified. Must use BDS for customisation.

### M.5.3 Departures / Relaxations Process

Deviation from PPDA Standard Bidding Documents requires written application to the PPDA Authority (Regulation 10 of PPDA Regulations 2014). The Authority issues its decision within 10 working days. The Authority may approve, approve with modifications, or reject the application.

## M.7 Worked Example -- Uganda

### Example: Assemble Tender Documents for UNRA District Road Upgrade (GoU Funded, Open Domestic Bidding)

**Project:** Upgrade of District Road Z from gravel to paved standard, 18 km.
**Procurement method:** Open Domestic Bidding (from Skill #47, value UGX 6 billion, GoU funded).
**Contract form:** FIDIC Red Book (Skill #42).

**Solution:**

**Step 1: SBD Selection**
- GoU funded + Open Domestic Bidding --> PPDA SBD for Works (Open Domestic Bidding edition)
- Verify current edition from PPDA Authority

**Step 2: Complete Bid Data Sheet**
- Employer: Uganda National Roads Authority (UNRA)
- Procurement reference: UNRA/WORKS/2026/[number]
- Bid validity: 90 days
- Bid security: 2% of estimated UGX 6 billion = UGX 120 million, unconditional bank guarantee
- Submission deadline: Allow minimum 30 working days from date of first advertisement
- Bid opening: same date, immediately after submission deadline, at UNRA offices
- Evaluation method: Technical compliance (pass/fail) then lowest evaluated price
- Currency: Uganda Shillings (UGX)

**Step 3: Draft Particular Conditions**
- Sub-Clause 4.2: Performance Security = 10% of Contract Price
- Sub-Clause 8.2: Time for Completion = 24 months (project-specific)
- Sub-Clause 8.7: Delay damages = 0.05% of Contract Price per day
- Sub-Clause 14.2: Advance Payment = 10% of Contract Price, repayable from 10% cumulative IPC
- Sub-Clause 14.7: Payment within 56 days (standard FIDIC, but note GoU cash flow reality)
- Sub-Clause 14.9: Retention = 5% to maximum 5% of Contract Price
- Sub-Clause 20.2: DAB = 1 member (contract < UGX 20 billion)
- Sub-Clause 20.6: Arbitration per UNCITRAL Rules, seat Kampala
- Applicable law: Laws of Uganda
- Language: English

**Step 4: Technical Specifications**
- Reference: MoWT General Specifications for Road and Bridge Works (current edition)
- Project-specific amendments: Addendum to standard specifications for project-specific materials, methods, and quality requirements
- Environmental management plan requirements included

**Step 5: Bill of Quantities**
- Prepared per Skill #51 (BoQ Preparation)
- Items match MoWT specification sections
- Measurement units consistent with standard specifications
- Preamble to BoQ specifies measurement conventions

**Step 6: Drawings List**
- All design drawings listed by number and title
- Separate volume for full-size drawings

**Step 7: Quality Check**

| Check | Status | Notes |
|-------|--------|-------|
| ITB text unmodified | PASS | All customisation via BDS |
| GCC text unmodified | PASS | All customisation via PCC |
| BDS-PCC consistency | PASS | Time, security, payment terms consistent |
| BoQ matches specifications | PASS | All items cross-checked |
| Drawings match BoQ | PASS | Quantities derivable from drawings |
| Bid security specified | PASS | UGX 120M, bank guarantee |
| Evaluation criteria stated | PASS | Technical compliance + lowest price |
| Minimum bidding period | PASS | 30+ working days allowed |

**Step 8: Approval**
- Submit to Contracts Committee for approval before issue
- Contracts Committee reviews and approves bidding documents

**Result:** Complete PPDA SBD for Works (ODB) with: BDS entries, FIDIC Red Book PCC, MoWT technical specifications, BoQ per Skill #51, drawings list, standard bid forms. Contracts Committee approval required before issue.
