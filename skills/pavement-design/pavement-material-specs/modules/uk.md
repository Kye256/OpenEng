### M.1.2 Institutional Context

UK uses the Specification for Highway Works (SHW, part of MCHW) for material specifications. This is a fundamentally different classification system from MoWT.

### M.1.3 Standard Philosophy

UK uses a performance-based specification approach with BS EN standards for testing. Materials are classified differently from MoWT (e.g., Type 1/Type 2 sub-base rather than CBR-based G-classes).

---

## M.3 Country-Specific Reasoning

UK material specifications use different classification systems: SHW Series 800 for unbound materials (Type 1, Type 2 sub-base), Series 700 for bituminous materials. Direct comparison with MoWT is not straightforward.
