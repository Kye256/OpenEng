### M.1.2 Institutional Context

UK uses DMRB HD 30 and HD 32 for pavement maintenance and rehabilitation assessment. SCANNER surveys (machine-based condition surveys) and SCRIM (skid resistance) data are standard. Fundamentally different assessment framework from MoWT.

---

## M.3 Country-Specific Reasoning

UK uses an automated network-level assessment (SCANNER) feeding into project-level investigation (coring, FWD, GPR). The assessment framework, condition parameters, and treatment selection process differ significantly from MoWT Vol 3 Part IV.
