### M.1.2 Institutional Context

ORN 31 is explicitly for "developing and emerging countries" (TRL). It is NOT used for pavement design in the UK, despite being published by TRL (a UK organisation). UK pavement design follows DMRB CD 227.

### M.1.3 Standard Philosophy

UK uses DMRB analytical approach with foundation classes and standardised designs. ORN 31 was developed by TRL for overseas application in tropical and sub-tropical environments.

## M.3 Country-Specific Reasoning

### M.3.1 Decision Logic (UK Branch)

ORN 31 is not applicable to UK pavement design. This module exists for completeness and comparison. UK engineers working on overseas projects may use ORN 31, but never for UK domestic projects.

### M.3.2 Professional Judgment (UK-Specific)

TRL developed ORN 31 based on decades of research in tropical countries. The knowledge is valuable for UK consultants working internationally but the design assumptions (climate, materials, construction quality) are fundamentally different from UK conditions.
