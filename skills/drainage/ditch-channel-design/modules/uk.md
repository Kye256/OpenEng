## M.3 Country-Specific Reasoning

### M.3.1 Decision Logic (UK Branch)

UK drainage often uses combined surface/subsurface systems with filter drains rather than open ditches. SuDS requirements should be considered for all new drainage design.

### M.3.2 Professional Judgment (UK-Specific)

**SuDS are a fundamental consideration in UK practice.** Swales (grass-lined channels designed to infiltrate and attenuate runoff) are preferred over hard-lined channels where ground conditions permit. Filter drains combine conveyance with infiltration.
