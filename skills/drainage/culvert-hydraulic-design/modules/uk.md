### M.1.2 Institutional Context

UK culvert design follows the same inlet/outlet control hydraulic principles. CIRIA C689 is the primary UK reference for culvert design and operation, covering hydraulic design, environmental considerations (including fish passage), and structural requirements. UK practice requires 1 in 100 year + climate change design events.

## M.2 Country-Specific Knowledge

UK uses the same hydraulic principles (inlet/outlet control) but with different entrance types (precast headwall units are standard), different minimum sizes, and environmental requirements including fish passage.

---

## M.3 Country-Specific Reasoning

**Environmental considerations are more prominent in UK practice.** Fish passage through culverts is a regulatory requirement in many situations. Natural bed culverts (with the invert set below stream bed level) are preferred. Ecological impact assessments are required.
